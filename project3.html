Electronic Fair

Exhibition Design
2020

ABOUT.

The Electronic Fair is an annual event held every April in Hong Kong, where we manage all aspects of the show. This trade show hosts thousands of B2B exhibitors and includes various forums and panels to facilitate business exchanges and connections. This year's edition featured vibrant electronic graphics to enhance the show's energetic atmosphere. The feature wall, which served as the main entrance decoration, created a strong, welcoming atmosphere for visitors and made a powerful statement about the event's dynamic nature.

ROLE.

I was the graphic designer for this project, responsible for creating the overall theme, developing graphics, and applying the visual identity across the venue. My role included conceptualizing the visual style, designing promotional materials, and ensuring a cohesive aesthetic experience throughout the event.

DESIGN
PROCESS.

During the creative process, I was inspired by the dynamic, electric wave patterns and other elements that highlight the beauty of electronic activities. By combining these elements, I created something vibrant and sharp to capture visitors' attention and draw them into our show with a fully immersive experience. We used appropriate graphics to decorate the venue, fully utilizing the space to create an atmosphere that we hope attendees will enjoy.
